Martian seen in the town

Joseph Baker - 01/03/2020

A Martian have been seen in the town in the afternoon of the 1st of March. Everybody was behaving as nothing happened, because everybody was thinking that it was just a costume. People started to suspect that he was a martian because the costume looked very real to be a fake and because he was talking in a weird and different language that nobody could understand.

People then, decided to call the police and in about an hour a police car appeared there. Policemen first thought that it was a joke made by kids because the could not believe there was a martian in the town. That was the reason why they lasted so much time in arrive there.

Policemen tried to speak to the martian as well but they could not understand what he was trying to say. After couple hours struggling then they decide to call a scientist specialized in paranormal phenomena.

The scientist, Doctor Norman Krugger, arrived there in two hours by helicopter with some of his equipment. After about half an hour working with a martian language's translator, Doctor Krugger said that the martian was "Asking for the toilet, cause it was a very long journey."
